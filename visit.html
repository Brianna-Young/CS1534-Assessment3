Aberdeen Maritime Museum

Learn about the rich history of Aberdeen’s relationship with the sea! Located on Shiprow, near the harbour, the maritime museum has displays showcasing the past, present and future of this coastal city. From fishing to shipping to offshore energy you can learn about it at the Maritime Museum!

Aberdeen Beach

Along the coast of Aberdeen is an expansive sandy shore beach. With a promenade from the harbour all the way to the river Don, this is a great spot for leisurely walks. Feel the slightly salty sea wind with excellent views of the water. Dotted along the beach to the north of the Don are pill boxes from the second world war, nearly swallowed by the sands. Excellent for walking dogs or taking romantic strolls, Aberdeen beach won’t disappoint.
